# M.S. Enterprises - Fintech Consultancy Website

A modern, responsive website for M.S. Enterprises featuring cutting-edge 3D animations, beautiful UI design, and comprehensive business information.

## 🌟 Features

### Pages
- **Homepage** - Hero section, services overview, features, statistics, technology stack, process, and CTA
- **About Us** - Company introduction, mission & vision, core values, team, timeline, and achievements  
- **Services** - Detailed accounting and technology services with pricing plans
- **Contact** - Contact form, business information, FAQ, office hours, and social media

### Design & Technology
- **Modern UI/UX** - Clean, professional design with gradient themes
- **3D Animations** - Three.js powered floating shapes and particle systems
- **Responsive Design** - Mobile-first approach with breakpoints for all devices
- **Performance Optimized** - Lazy loading, GPU acceleration, and efficient animations
- **Interactive Elements** - Hover effects, scroll animations, and smooth transitions
