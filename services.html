Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. A simple website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while complex applications can take 3-6 months. We provide detailed timelines during the planning phase.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes, we offer comprehensive support and maintenance packages. Our support includes bug fixes, updates, security patches, and performance monitoring.

Can you work with our existing systems?

Absolutely! We specialize in integrating new solutions with existing systems. Our team will analyze your current infrastructure and ensure seamless integration.

What technologies do you use?

We use modern, industry-standard technologies including React, Vue.js, Node.js, Python, AWS, Azure, and more. We choose the best technology stack for each specific project.
