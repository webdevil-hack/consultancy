FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly can you start working on my project?

We can typically start within 2-3 business days after the initial consultation and agreement. For urgent projects, we can expedite the process.

What are your payment terms?

We typically work with a 50% advance and 50% on completion for project-based work. For monthly services, we require payment at the beginning of each month.

Do you provide support after project completion?

Yes! We provide 30 days of free support after project completion. Extended support and maintenance packages are also available.

Can I schedule a free consultation?

Absolutely! We offer free initial consultations to understand your needs and provide recommendations. Contact us to schedule yours.

What industries do you work with?

We work with businesses across all industries including retail, healthcare, real estate, manufacturing, education, hospitality, and more.
